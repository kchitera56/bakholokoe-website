About Bakholokoe Game Reserve

Where nature, culture, and wildlife come alive at the Harrismith Botanic Gardens.

Wildlife & Biodiversity

Bakholokoe Game Reserve is home to spectacular wildlife roaming freely across the rich grasslands of the Harrismith Botanic Gardens. Visitors can encounter:

  • ✔ Antelope: Eland, Springbok, Blesbok, Impala
  • ✔ Diverse Birdlife: Raptors, forest birds, wetland species
  • ✔ Small mammals, reptiles, and rare plant species

Scenic Nature Trails

Surrounded by pine forests, rocky plateaus, waterfalls, and viewpoints, Bakholokoe Game Reserve offers unforgettable hiking and photography experiences. Trails range from easy walks to moderate mountain adventures.

Cultural Heritage of the Bakholokoe People

The reserve honours the heritage of one of Harrismith's earliest communities, the Bakholokoe people. Visitors can explore:

  • ✔ Traditional storytelling and ancestral sites
  • ✔ Lineage and origins of the Bakholokoe people
  • ✔ Their historic relationship with the land and wildlife
